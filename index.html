ABOUT

Bluematters is a pan-European provider of unique resourcing solutions. In the complex world of sourcing skilled personnel, we can deliver. We source and deploy experienced teams to projects across multiple sectors. Our team uses their knowledge of numerous industries to source the right personnel with the right competencies to deliver your project. We will work with you to understand what success means to you, and we’ll adapt our model to deliver on those success crtieria.

SERVICES

We provide a range of blue-collar as well as source skilled and accredited personnel such as electricians, commissioners, nurses and healthcare workers. We have in-depth knowledge of multiple industries, and will use this expertise to supply you with a perfect team. We will find the resources, manage their leave, development and payroll, vet them and organise the paperwork – so you don’t have to employ them directly.
